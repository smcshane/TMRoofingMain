Before & After - Barn Roof

This barn was in major need of a reroof. We salvaged the old peg tiles, and carefully removed the old fittings and battern. We repaired some rotten rafters and fitted a breathable membrane, which we battened over. The new bonnets and ridge tiles were fitted with mortar.

Before & After - Lead Work

This property in Hastings had a water leak into the room below. We hacked back the render, ground the new lead flashings into the brickwork, and properly rendered the exposed brickwork. A new chimney pot was fitted, and chimney and firewalls were coated with FIX-R all weather roof coat

Before & After - Commercial Cladding

This client ran a local commercial repair shop with some very old and damaged cladding on their workshop. My team removed the damaged cladding and fitted new timber supports which were bolted to the main steel girders. We also fitted new cladding, end capping and spotlights.

Before & After - Lead Gulley

This property had a lead gulley between three pitched roofs. The lead had split in several places and was allowing water ingress into the bedroom below. We replaced this defective gulley for a lovely GRP system which is guaranteed for 25 years.
